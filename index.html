Poultry Feed Ration Calculator

This application will help you calculate the daily required feed intake for your poultry birds. It will also give estimated cummulative consumption for a period of time (from day-old to maturity). You can download a PDF file of your birds' feed ration. Click on 'Calculate Feed' to calculate your birds' feed ration.

Layers

Features

  • Raised for egg production
  • May start laying from 18 weeks
  • Up to 2kg live weight after 20 weeks
  • Routine vaccination required
  • Can live up to 3 years but stops production after 24 months

Features
